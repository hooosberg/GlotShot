# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **GlotShot** ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

## Data Collection and Usage

**GlotShot** is a local macOS application. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- **Local Processing**: All image processing, text generation, and file handling occur locally on your device.
- **No Analytics**: We do not track your usage or sends analytics data to any third-party servers.
- **No Account Required**: You do not need to create an account or log in to use the application.

## AI Features (Ollama)

If you strictly opt-in to use the AI translation features:
- The application connects to a **local instance** of Ollama running on your machine (typically at `http://localhost:11434`).
- Your text data is sent only to this local instance for processing.
- No data is sent to external cloud servers.
